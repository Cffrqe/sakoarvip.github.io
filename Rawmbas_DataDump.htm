Our Delicious Flavors

Three incredible muffins, each with a unique and satisfying taste.

Apple Raspberry

View Ingredients

Apples (fresh with skin), gluten-free all-purpose flour (garbanzo bean flour, potato starch, tapioca flour, white sorghum flour, fava bean flour), unsweetened raspberries, raw cane sugar, gluten-free steel-cut oats, grapeseed oil, xanthan gum, baking powder, Himalayan salt, baking soda.

Allergen statement

Contains: garbanzo beans that are manufactured in a facility that uses tree nuts, soy and sesame seeds.

Storage

Keep frozen. Shelf life in freezer 3 months. Shelf life from frozen including thaw time in ambient setting 72 hours.

Bountiful Blueberry

View Ingredients

Apples (fresh with skin), gluten-free steel-cut oat flour, raw cane sugar, blueberries, grapeseed oil, flax meal, gluten-free all-purpose flour (garbanzo bean flour, potato starch, tapioca flour, white sorghum flour, fava bean flour), rolled oats, baking powder, xanthan gum, cinnamon, Himalayan salt, baking soda.

Allergen statement

Contains: garbanzo beans that are manufactured in a facility that uses tree nuts, soy and sesame seeds.

Storage

Keep frozen. Shelf life in freezer 3 months. Shelf life from frozen including thaw time in ambient setting 72 hours.

Double Chocolate Chip

View Ingredients

Apples (fresh with skin), gluten-free all-purpose flour (garbanzo bean flour, potato starch, tapioca flour, white sorghum flour, fava bean flour), gluten-free steel-cut oats, raw cane sugar, dark chocolate chips (brown sugar, cocoa powder, cocoa butter), grapeseed oil, cacao, xanthan gum, baking powder, Himalayan salt, baking soda, cinnamon.

Allergen statement

Contains: garbanzo beans that are manufactured in a facility that uses tree nuts, soy and sesame seeds.

Storage

Keep frozen. Shelf life in freezer 3 months. Shelf life from frozen including thaw time in ambient setting 72 hours.

Our Story, Baked with Passion

An Indigenous family-owned company, established on Vancouver Island with the goal of providing delicious, gluten-free, vegan muffins made with the highest quality ingredients available. Quality is ensured as every Rawmbas muffin originates in our local state-of-the-art production facility.

Recognized by many as the best muffins in the marketplace today, we pour our heart into every batch, ensuring a purely satisfying experience for you.
